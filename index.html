About Zu Peshawar BRT

Peshawar Bus Rapid Transit, known as Zu, is a modern and efficient public transport system designed to serve the city of Peshawar. Our goal is to provide a safe, reliable, and comfortable commuting experience for all residents and visitors.

The Zu service operates on dedicated bus lanes, ensuring smooth and rapid travel, even during peak hours. Our fleet of state-of-the-art buses is equipped with modern amenities and provides easy access for people with disabilities. We are committed to reducing traffic congestion and promoting a greener environment by encouraging the use of public transport.
